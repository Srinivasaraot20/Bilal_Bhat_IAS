The Journey of Resilience

Dr. Bilal Mohi-Ud-Din Bhat IAS's story is one of extraordinary perseverance and determination. Born in November 1985 in Srinagar, Kashmir, he hails from Haripora Younis village in the Handwara district of North Kashmir, an area deeply affected by militancy and conflict.

Growing up in such challenging circumstances, Dr. Bilal's journey to success was far from easy. His father, Ghulam Mohiuddin Bhat, a retired KAS officer, served as both inspiration and guide, instilling in him the values of public service and dedication to the nation.

Despite the odds stacked against him, Dr. Bilal's unwavering commitment to his goals and his passion for learning propelled him forward. His success in securing AIR-10 in UPSC 2016, achieved in his fourth attempt, stands as a testament to the power of persistence and hard work.

Today, as an Indian Forest Officer and mentor to thousands of UPSC aspirants, Dr. Bilal continues to inspire others with his story of triumph over adversity, proving that with determination, any dream is achievable.

He is one of the most popular officers in Jammu & Kashmir.

Philosophy & Beliefs

Dr. Bilal's approach to life and success is deeply rooted in his belief that persistence and continuous learning are the keys to overcoming any obstacle. His journey from a conflict-affected region to the highest echelons of civil services exemplifies this philosophy.

As a mentor, he emphasizes the importance of not just academic excellence but also character building and social responsibility. His guidance extends beyond exam preparation to holistic personality development.

Rooted in Islamic values and inspired by his father's legacy of public service, Dr. Bilal believes in giving back to society and helping others achieve their dreams, just as he achieved his own.

"Success is not just about personal achievement, but about lifting others up and contributing to the betterment of society. Every challenge is an opportunity to grow stronger."

- Dr. Bilal Mohi-Ud-Din Bhat IAS
